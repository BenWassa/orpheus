# Product

## Register
product

## Users
Individuals analyzing their own Spotify listening history to reflect on their emotional state and life themes.

## Product Purpose
A personal music analysis tool that surfaces emotional and thematic patterns from listening data. It visualizes valence, arousal, depth, and themes, turning data into a meaningful mirror for self-reflection that feels like reading a letter about yourself rather than a corporate analytics dashboard.

## Brand Personality
Observational, intuitive, explorable, minimal, personal, and reflective.

## Anti-references
Corporate analytics tools, overwhelming data dashboards, dense spreadsheets, generic SaaS apps.

## Design Principles
- Observational, not judgmental
- Read-in-five-minutes narrative
- Personal, letter-like feel
- Data as a mirror, not a spreadsheet

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard web accessibility, ensuring contrast and clear text readability for data visualizations.
